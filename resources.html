Godot Script

The Godot Engine's Built-In Programming Language!

GDScript is a high-level, dynamically typed programming language specifically designed for the Godot game engine. It's known for its ease of use, beginner-friendliness, and seamless integration with Godot's features.

Simple and Readable

GDScript has a clean and concise syntax, making it easy to learn and understand.

Integrated with Godot

GDScript is tightly integrated with Godot's engine, providing direct access to game objects, scenes, and other core features.
